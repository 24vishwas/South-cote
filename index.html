    South Cote - Family Home

    Nestled in the heart of Mysore, South Cote offers a unique blend of traditional hospitality and modern comfort, designed for families. Our home is more than just a stay; it's an experience where every detail is tailored to your needs. Whether you're looking to indulge in local cuisine prepared by us or wish to cook a family meal in our optional kitchen setups, we have you covered. At SouthCote, we believe in creating memories. Spend evenings by our bonfire, sharing stories under the stars, or explore the rich culture of Mysore with a personalized tourist guide service. We accommodate not just your family, but also your vehicles, with ample parking for buses and cars. Discover the charm of Mysore with us!
